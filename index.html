Nigeria

Nigeria is a country in West Africa. It is the most populous country in Africa, known for its diverse culture and vibrant economy. Nigeria has over 250 ethnic groups, with the Hausa, Yoruba, and Igbo being the largest. The capital city is Abuja, and Lagos is the largest city and a major financial hub.
